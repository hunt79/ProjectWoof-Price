Pug

The pug is a small dog breed. It has a square body and wrinkled face. It was brought to Europe from China. Pugs are calm dogs and are very rarely aggresive. Pugs arrived in the U.S. in the 19th century.

Frank is a pug in Men and Black

Not hypoallergenic

Life span:12-15yrs

Origin:China

The pug may have health problem. Due to its wrinkled face it could have sereval eye injuries. It is common for pugs to have obiesty if not exercised enough. Since the pug has a square and short body it has breathing problems. Pugs can also suffer from necrotizing meningoencephalitis which is an inflammation in the brain
